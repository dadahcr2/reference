**Using rerolls to obtain epic parts**

- If you don't obtain any Epic Parts from a Common, Uncommon or Rare Chest, or you obtain the minumum amount of Epic Parts from an Epic(6), Champion(12), or Legendary(12) Chest, it is possible to obtain more Epic Parts by using a Reroll
- It turns out that using rerolls for Rare Chest, followed by Uncommon Chests, are the least costly.
